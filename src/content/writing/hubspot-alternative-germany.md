# Best HubSpot Alternatives for German SMEs

HubSpot's market share among German SMEs is real and defensible — the product is functional, well-documented, and increasingly GDPR-configurable. The problem for many German companies isn't that HubSpot is bad. It's that HubSpot's default configuration requires active remediation to be GDPR-compliant, it transfers data to US servers unless EU residency is explicitly activated, and its pricing in EUR at mid-tier can reach €500–1,500/month for features that European alternatives provide for €50–200/month.

For German SMEs that need CRM + marketing automation + GDPR confidence, the question is whether HubSpot's advantages justify its cost and configuration complexity vs. the alternatives.

This comparison covers 6 alternatives with data residency verification for each.

---

## Why German Companies Look for HubSpot Alternatives

**Data residency by default vs. by configuration.** HubSpot's EU data residency option stores contact and company data in Frankfurt (AWS EU-WEST-1). But this requires: a HubSpot Professional or Enterprise plan (not available on Starter), active selection during account setup, and verification that all HubSpot add-ons and integrations are also configured for EU data residency. The default HubSpot account stores data in the US. This requires attention that many companies don't apply during onboarding.

**Data sharing with HubSpot for product improvement.** HubSpot's default settings include sharing contact interaction data with HubSpot for product improvement purposes. Under the German DPA guidance on data minimization (GDPR Art. 5(1)(c)), this requires either explicit consent or a very well-documented legitimate interest. The setting can be disabled, but it must be actively disabled — it's not disabled by default.

**Pricing structure.** HubSpot's pricing model is: free (genuinely limited), Starter (~€45/month), Professional (~€890/month). The gap between Starter and Professional is large, and Professional is where most SME marketing automation needs (custom workflows, advanced segmentation, A/B testing, reporting) are met. This creates a pricing cliff that German alternatives don't have.

## The 6 Alternatives

### 1. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — Best for Email-Led Marketing Automation

**Data residency:** 🟢 EU — French company, data centers in Germany and France by default  
**DPA/AVV:** Available as standard (no request required)  
**Pricing (EUR):** Free, €19/month (Starter), €49/month (Business), custom (Enterprise)  
**Marketing automation:** Yes — visual workflow builder, behavioral triggers, lead scoring  
**CRM:** Basic — contact stages, deal pipeline (simpler than HubSpot)  
**German language:** Full interface localization, German customer support  
**Best for:** Email-first marketing automation for teams where email is the primary marketing channel  
**Limitation:** CRM pipeline management is lighter than HubSpot; no ad retargeting integration  

**Migration from HubSpot:** Contact export from HubSpot → CSV → Brevo import. Preserve consent documentation in custom fields. Email templates need to be rebuilt. Timeline: 2–3 days.

---

### 2. ActiveCampaign — Best for Advanced Email Automation

**Data residency:** 🟡 Yellow — US company; EU data residency available (must be selected during signup)  
**DPA/AVV:** Available  
**Pricing (EUR):** ~€29/month (Starter), ~€49/month (Plus), ~€149/month (Professional)  
**Marketing automation:** Strong — one of the most capable email automation builders available  
**CRM:** Included — CRM + deals included in all plans, not an add-on  
**German language:** Partial localization; English-primary support  
**Best for:** Teams that need sophisticated email automation (conditional logic, multi-branch sequences) without HubSpot Professional pricing  
**Limitation:** EU data residency requires active selection; English-primary support may be a friction point  

---

### 3. Salesforce Essentials — Best for Enterprise-Bound SMEs

**Data residency:** 🟡 Yellow — US company; EU data centers available (Frankfurt); DPA available  
**DPA/AVV:** Available  
**Pricing (EUR):** ~€30/user/month (Essentials), ~€90/user/month (Professional)  
**Marketing automation:** Requires Salesforce Marketing Cloud Engagement add-on (~€400+/month additional); base Essentials is CRM only  
**CRM:** Market-leading CRM functionality  
**German language:** Full localization, German support  
**Best for:** Companies that know they'll grow into Salesforce (200+ person companies, enterprise sales)  
**Limitation:** Marketing automation is not native to Essentials tier; total cost for CRM + automation is higher than HubSpot Professional  

---

### 4. Zoho CRM + Zoho Campaigns — Best for Budget-Constrained Teams

**Data residency:** 🟡 Yellow — Indian company; EU data center (Netherlands); DPA available  
**DPA/AVV:** Available  
**Pricing (EUR):** Zoho CRM ~€14–42/user/month; Zoho Campaigns free–€6/month  
**Marketing automation:** Moderate — Zoho Campaigns handles email automation; Zoho CRM workflow automation separate  
**CRM:** Functional but interface is less polished than HubSpot  
**German language:** Interface localization available; support quality variable  
**Best for:** Budget-constrained teams willing to invest setup time for cost savings  
**Limitation:** EU data center is in the Netherlands, not Germany — may not satisfy some German company data residency requirements  

---

### 5. SuperOffice — Best for Scandinavian/German Mittelstand

**Data residency:** 🟢 Green — Norwegian company, EU-hosted, GDPR compliance built-in  
**DPA/AVV:** Available as standard  
**Pricing (EUR):** ~€45/user/month (CRM Online), custom (Enterprise)  
**Marketing automation:** Basic — primarily a CRM with email marketing; advanced automation limited  
**CRM:** Strong B2B CRM with good contact management  
**German language:** Full localization, German support available  
**Best for:** German B2B companies that prioritize GDPR Green status and want EU-based vendor  
**Limitation:** Marketing automation capabilities are limited compared to HubSpot or Brevo  

---

### 6. Evalanche — Best for German-Language Email Marketing at Scale

**Data residency:** 🟢 Green — German company (SC-Networks GmbH, Grasbrunn bei München), ISO-certified data centers in Germany  
**DPA/AVV:** Standard German DPA agreement  
**Pricing (EUR):** ~€200–1,000/month (volume-dependent)  
**Marketing automation:** Strong email automation, landing pages, A/B testing  
**CRM:** Limited — email-focused platform, not a full CRM  
**German language:** Native — German product, German support  
**Best for:** German companies with high email volume requirements (50,000+ contacts) that need guaranteed German data residency and German-native product  
**Limitation:** Higher entry price than alternatives; not a full CRM; primarily for established email programs

## Migration Path from HubSpot

**Step 1:** Export contacts with all custom fields and consent data. HubSpot: Contacts > Export All. Include: contact properties, lifecycle stage, email subscription status, notes (last 12 months).

**Step 2:** Map consent fields to the target platform's consent management structure. Every platform has different consent field architecture. Do this mapping before import.

**Step 3:** Do not import contacts without documented consent status. Separate your export into "confirmed opt-in" and "other" lists. Import only confirmed opt-in contacts to the active marketing database.

**Step 4:** Rebuild email templates in the target platform. Do not rely on exports from HubSpot — template formats are platform-specific.

**Step 5:** Reconnect integrations. Any integration with other tools (analytics, Zapier/Make, website forms) needs to be reconnected to the new platform.

Migration timeline: 3–6 weeks for a typical SME (under 5,000 contacts, 5–10 active workflows).
