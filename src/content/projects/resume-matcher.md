## Why it exists

Applying in Germany is a language and evidence problem as much as a qualification problem.
The same experience can read senior or vague depending on how tightly it maps to the job
description, the local role vocabulary, and the proof points a recruiter expects to see.

resume-matcher is a small tool for closing that gap. It does not write a fantasy CV. It
compares the actual CV against a real role and shows where the evidence is missing, weak,
or buried.

## Current status

The live workflow is used for role-by-role CV tailoring during Berk's 2026 search. The
next version will add reusable application templates for German senior marketing roles:
growth, brand communications, integrated marketing, and AI marketing transformation.

## What I learned

The useful part is not "AI writes your CV." The useful part is structured judgment:
which parts of your experience match the role, which claims need stronger evidence, and
which keywords belong because they reflect real work you have done.
