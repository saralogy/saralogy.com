A demo recorded in one session, deliberately kept unpolished. The song came from thinking about all the things that don't exist yet—products, ideas, versions of yourself—and the messy work of building them.

The lyrics are repetitive by design. The chorus doesn't resolve; it just restates the tension. And then it ends.

This is the demo version. I've had offers to re-record it, clean it up, add layers. I'm not doing that. The imperfection is the whole point.

Listen on SoundCloud if you want the raw version. If you want the finished version, that doesn't exist yet. Maybe it will. Maybe the prototype is all there is.
